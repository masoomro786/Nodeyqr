Universal Studios Hollywood's Nighttime Lights at Hogwarts Castle returning in March

Universal Studios Hollywood announced that the ever-popular Nighttime Lights at Hogwarts Castle at Los Angeles' Wizarding World of Harry Potter will return for the first three weeks of March, and the entire final week of next month. The first night kicks off this Saturday Universal Studios Hollywood springs into March with the return of the dazzling light projection spectacular, “The Nighttime Lights at Hogwarts Castle,” at “The Wizarding World of Harry Potter” for a limited engagement.
